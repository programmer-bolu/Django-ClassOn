Frequently Asked Questions

How do I join a class?

To join a class, simply sign up for an account and search for your class using the class code provided by your administrator.

Can I view schedules from multiple classes?

Yes, you can join multiple classes and view schedules for each one within your dashboard.

How do admins post schedules?

Admins can log in to their account, go to the admin panel, and post or update class schedules as needed.

Is there a mobile app available?

Currently, ClassGr is accessible through our web platform, and we are working on developing a mobile app.

How can I contact support?

If you need assistance, please visit our contact page and fill out the form, or email us at support@classgr.com.
